Medicare Basics

What is Medicare and who qualifies?

Medicare is a federal health insurance program for people who are:

  • 65 years or older or
  • Receiving disability benefits for at least 24 months or
  • Have End-Stage Renal Disease requiring dialysis or a kidney transplant or
  • Diagnosed with Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis (ALS)

Does Original
Medicare cover my
prescription drugs?

No.

However, you may add a Medicare Part D prescription plan or enroll in a medicare Advantage plsn (part C) that may include prescription coverage.
