    What is Medicare?
    Medicare is the federal health insurance program for:
  • People who are 65 or older
  • People recieving disability benefits for at least 24 months
  • People with End-Stage Renal Disease requiring dialysis or a kidney transplant
  • People with Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis (ALS)
    • What are my Medicare options?
    Once your Medicare begins you can choose:
  • Original Medicare + Part D Prescription Plan
  • Original Medicare + Medicare Supplement + Part D Prescription Plan
  • Original Medicare + Part C Advantage Plan which includes Part D Prescription Plan
    • What does Medicare cover?
    Part A covers:
  • Inpatient hospital care
  • Skilled nursing facility care
  • Some home health care
  • Hospice
    • Part B covers:
  • Outpatient Medical services such as doctor's visits, preventative care and diagnostic tests
  • Ambulance services
  • Durable medical equipment (DME)
  • Inpatient / Outpatient mental health service
